# emotional-time-travel
Interactive prototype demonstrating Emotional Time Travel, developed by Indira Merritt
## Live Protype
https://indiramerri.github.io/emotional-time-travel/
## About
Emotional Time Travel is an interactive experience based on my Capacity Architecture framework.  Rather than immediately asking users to analyze or change their emotions, the experience guides them to recognize where they are in time-visiting the past for context, return to the present to identify current capacity , or design the futurethrough intentional practice.
## How It Works 
-Visit the Past to recognize adaptive patterns.
-Stay Present to identify and practice a current capacity. 
-Design the Fuure by choosing a direction and the capacity it requires. 
-Complete the experience with a Capacity Return.
